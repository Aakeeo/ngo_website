Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Reminiscence Welfare Trust respects your privacy and is committed to protecting personal information shared with us.

1. Information We Collect

When you make a donation or interact with our website, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Contact number
  • Donation amount
  • Payment transaction details

2. Purpose of Data Collection

Collected information is used to:

  • Process donations securely
  • Issue donation receipts and confirmations
  • Maintain donor records for compliance purposes
  • Communicate updates related to our initiatives

3. Payment Security

All payments are processed through Razorpay, a secure payment gateway. We do not store credit/debit card or banking information on our servers.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal information. Data may be shared only with:

  • Payment gateway partners (Razorpay) for processing transactions
  • Regulatory or legal authorities, if required by law

5. Data Protection

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect personal data from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. Our security measures include:

  • Secure HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure payment processing through certified payment gateways
  • Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis

6. Consent

By using this website or making a donation, you consent to this Privacy Policy and the collection, use, and sharing of your information as described herein.
